About CommandlineOS

Founded in 2020 by a team of experienced DevOps engineers and full-stack developers, CommandlineOS emerged from a simple observation: teams were wasting countless hours managing disparate tools and writing repetitive scripts. We realized there had to be a better way. Our founding team had collectively spent over 50 years building enterprise software, and we channeled that expertise into creating a platform that would revolutionize how teams interact with their development environments. What started as an internal tool quickly became indispensable, and we knew we had to share it with the world.

Today, CommandlineOS is driven by a core mission: to empower development teams to work smarter, not harder. We believe that great tools should be intuitive, powerful, and accessible to teams of all sizes. Our commitment to innovation, security, and customer success has made us the trusted partner for hundreds of companies worldwide. We're not just building software; we're building a community of developers who are passionate about efficiency, automation, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible with command-line interfaces.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CommandlineOS and how it can transform your workflow.

CommandlineOS is a comprehensive platform that enables teams to build, customize, and deploy enterprise-grade command-line interfaces without extensive coding. It works by providing a modular framework where you define commands, arguments, and actions through intuitive configuration. Our SDK and API handle the heavy lifting, allowing your team to focus on business logic rather than CLI infrastructure. The platform manages versioning, distribution, team collaboration, and security—everything needed to run professional CLIs at scale.

Most teams can have their first CLI up and running within hours, not weeks. Our quick-start templates and comprehensive documentation get you started immediately. Simple CLIs can be deployed in 30 minutes, while more complex enterprise solutions typically take 1-2 days. The modular architecture means you can start with basic functionality and incrementally add features as needed. Our onboarding team is also available to help guide you through the process and ensure optimal setup for your specific use case.

Security is at the core of CommandlineOS. We implement enterprise-grade encryption for data in transit and at rest, role-based access control (RBAC) for granular permission management, comprehensive audit logging for compliance, and regular security audits by third-party firms. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant and support single sign-on (SSO) for enterprise deployments. All communications are encrypted with TLS 1.3, and we maintain strict data isolation between customer accounts. Additionally, we provide API key rotation, webhook signing, and IP whitelisting capabilities for maximum security.

Absolutely. CommandlineOS is designed for seamless integration. We provide comprehensive REST APIs and SDKs for Python, JavaScript, Go, and Java. We have pre-built integrations with popular tools like GitHub, GitLab, Slack, Jira, and AWS. Our webhook system allows you to trigger actions in external systems, and you can call external APIs from your CLI commands. We also support custom integrations through our plugin architecture. Our integration specialists can help you connect CommandlineOS with your specific tech stack and ensure smooth data flow between systems.

Professional and Enterprise plans include priority email support, Slack integration, dedicated account managers, and 1-hour response times for critical issues. All plans include access to our knowledge base with video tutorials, best practices guides, and code examples. Enterprise customers also get on-site training, custom development support, and quarterly business reviews.
